What Causes Hair Loss and How Can It Be Prevented?

Hair loss can be due to genetics, hormonal changes, medical conditions, medications, nutritional deficiencies, and stress. To prevent hair loss, eat a diet rich in iron, zinc, and vitamins, avoid excessive styling and harsh chemicals, and manage stress with yoga and meditation.
